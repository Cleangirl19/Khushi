Why Collagen Serums Work

Collagen is the protein that keeps skin firm and plump — but our bodies produce less of it after our mid-20s. A good collagen serum helps stimulate your skin's natural production and provides hydration at a deeper level. Pair with Vitamin C to boost collagen synthesis even further.
